Every footprint on the board, its source, and its dimensions ‚Äî so each one is
**verifiable**, not "trust me." Two classes:

1. **Vendor footprints** (6 parts) ‚Äî the official manufacturer land patterns from the

2. **Generic footprints** (the rest) ‚Äî generated by [`generate_sch.py`](generate_sch.py)
   to **IPC-7351B density-B (nominal)** land patterns. Dimensions tabulated below.

## Verifying in Fusion
After import, in the PCB editor you can measure any pad: select it ‚Üí the Inspect panel
shows size/position, or use the **Measure** tool between pads to confirm pitch against the
tables above.
